About the 2026 Jog-a-thon

The jog-a-thon is Pacific Trails School’s main fundraiser for the year, focused on community, movement, and support for every classroom.

What students will do

Each grade level will have a 20–30 minute running session on the school track. Students are encouraged to jog, run, or walk at their own pace. Laps are tracked using RFID tags so every student’s effort is counted fairly.

Funds raised will support technology upgrades, library resources, and campus improvements selected by the school administration.

Frequently asked questions

A few common questions from families about how the jog-a-thon works.

How are laps tracked?

Each student wears an RFID tag that automatically records a lap every time they pass the timing line. This keeps counting fair and accurate for all students.

Can I donate a flat amount instead of per lap?

Yes. The online donation page lets you choose a flat donation or a per-lap pledge. Per-lap pledges are calculated after the event based on actual laps run.

Are donations tax-deductible?

In most cases, yes. You will receive a confirmation email with receipt details once your donation is processed. Please consult your tax advisor for specifics.

What should my student bring on event day?

Comfortable running shoes, a labeled water bottle, sunscreen if needed, and appropriate clothing for outdoor activity. The school will share any additional guidance in advance.
