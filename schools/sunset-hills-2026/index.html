About the 2026 Jog-a-thon

The jog-a-thon is our biggest fundraiser of the year! All proceeds go directly to upgrading our playground, supporting the music program, and funding after-school activities.

What students will do

Each class will take turns running on the school track for 30 minutes. Students can jog, walk, or run at their own pace. Every lap is tracked with our RFID system, so every student's effort is counted fairly!

This year's fundraising goal is $25,000. Help us reach it!

Frequently asked questions

Common questions from parents about the jog-a-thon.

How does RFID lap tracking work?

Each student gets a lightweight RFID wristband. When they cross the timing mat, it automatically records a lap. No scanning needed — it's fully automatic!

Can I donate a flat amount instead of per lap?

Absolutely! You can choose either a flat donation or a per-lap pledge on the donation page. Per-lap pledges are calculated after the event.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! You'll receive an email receipt with tax-deduction information. Our 501(c)(3) status makes all donations tax-deductible.

What should my student bring?

Comfortable athletic shoes, a labeled water bottle, and sunscreen. Dress for outdoor activity — we'll be outside unless it rains.

Is there a rain date?

Yes! If it rains on April 25, the event moves to April 26. We'll send a morning update if there are any schedule changes.
